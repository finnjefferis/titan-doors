Composite Doors

Composite doors crafted by the UK's leading suppliers

Titan composite doors are engineered to suit every budget and specification. Our range starts with robust GRP (Glass Reinforced Plastic) doors offering outstanding value, steps up through solid timber core options for enhanced thermal performance and security, and extends to high-end aluminium and biometric door systems for those seeking the very best.

Available in a wide range of styles, colours and glass options, every Titan door is made to measure and tailored to your exact specifications. Whether you're upgrading a single entrance or fitting out an entire development, we have the solution.
